What we do

A builder and an operator — not just an investor.

Most firms either build products or hold companies. We do both. We create technology businesses in-house, and we stay to run them — because durable value comes from operating well, not just owning. Revenue funds the next build.

01 — Build

We start from the problem

We look for boring, persistent problems — the kind businesses live with every day because no one has bothered to solve them properly. Then we build software that quietly makes them disappear.

02 — Operate

A launched product is the start, not the finish. We operate what we build: iterating with real customers, earning real revenue, and compounding each business into something durable.
